Urban Recipe - Recipe Builder Concept


Last spring, I worked with a team to create an app concept for Urban Recipe, an Atlanta-based organization that provides co-op programs to those in need of food. This app would allow members of Urban Recipe's co-op program to receive nutrional counsel by suggesting healthy recipes based on the items in the user's virtual pantry.
